## Featured Projects

### Software Engineering Agile League (SEAL)


An academic hackathon management and research platform for the annual **Software Engineering Agile League**, organized by the Software Engineering Department and PDP at FPT University HCMC.

- Supports seasonal hackathons and multi-round competitions, including preliminary and final rounds.
- Manages events, participating teams, submissions, judges, evaluation criteria, scoring, and competition results.
- Accommodates FPT University teams, mixed-university teams, and teams from partner universities.
- Improves transparency and reduces errors caused by manual event-management workflows.
- Collects structured judging data to support research into **inter-rater reliability** and scoring consistency in software engineering competitions.

**Tech Stack:** Spring Boot, React, SQL Server, JWT, RESTful APIs, Swagger API

### [Air Quality Lab Care](https://github.com/tunkiztnt/JavaServlet_AirQualityLabCare)

A web-based air-quality monitoring system designed for laboratories and classrooms.

- Manages rooms, sensors, pollutants, threshold rules, alerts, and audit logs.
- Provides dashboards and reports for monitoring environmental conditions.
- Supports role-based access control for administrators, lab managers, technicians, and viewers.
- Imports air-quality readings from UCI and OpenAQ CSV datasets.

**Tech Stack:** Java Servlet/JSP, JSTL, JDBC, SQL Server, Apache Tomcat
